Lorde Lounges in Bikini in New Oceanic Feeling Music Video Watch Now!

Publish date: 2024-07-16

Lorde has a new music video!

A year after dropping her latest album Solar Power, the 25-year-old Grammy-winning singer released the music video for her song “Oceanic Feeling.”

In the video – which Lorde co-directed with Joel Kefali – Lorde lounges out by the sea while wearing a patterned red bikini before hopping on a boat and rowing away from the shore.

“Now the cherry-black lipstick’s gathering dust in a drawer / I don’t need her anymore / ‘Cause I got this power,” Lorde sings. “I just had to breathe (Breathe out, breathe out, breathe out, breathe out) / And tune in (Tune in, tune in, tune in, tune in) / I just had to breathe (Breathe out, breathe out, breathe out, breathe out) / And tune in (Tune in, tune in, tune in, tune in).”
