Publish date: 2024-09-14

The ultimate feel-good song for your booty, “Juicy,” was given a music video and a few new lines on Aug. 15, 2019. Doja Cat enlisted Tyga’s rhymes for the remix, and they made a dynamic duo in this music video that was more colorful than a box of Crayola Crayons! Doja bared her derriere in one outfit after another, like a purple string bikini layered with a transparent fur-lined coat and a figure-hugging watermelon jumpsuit. Tyga also stunted in equally eccentric looks, like graphic-print sweats and a bowling shirt plastered with lightning bolts. 

Doja ditched clothes altogether for the opening shot of the video (a giant cherry blocking the rapper’s derriere still made the video meet YouTube guidelines). The video was a wild ride of colors, outfits and rhymes from start to finish, befitting the lyrics to Doja’s track! “I keep it juicy juicy, I eat that lunch (yeah) / She keep that booty booty, she keep that plump (yeah yeah) / That natural beauty beauty, yeah, yeah,” she raps in the opening verse.
