How did ABC start his Professional Career?

Mr. Rhoden appeared in numerous self-documentary movies in addition to being a bodybuilder. Inspired by Mr. Yohnnie Shambourger, the former Mr. Universe, Shawn started bodybuilding in 1992.

After years of training, Shawn competed in his first amateur bodybuilding event in 2009. At the 1999 IFBB North American Championship, he came in third place in the Amateurs category.

Later, he took part in a variety of professional competitions, such as the 2014 IFBB Arnold Classic Europe, the 2011 Mr. Olympia, and the 2010 IFFBB Dallas Europa Super Show, among others.

But in 2018, his dream was realized when he defeated many top professional bodybuilders to win Mr. Olympia. He had an appearance in the renowned documentary series Mr. Olympia in 2013, which follows professional bodybuilders as they train for and compete in the Mr. Olympia competition.

He competed in the 49th Annual Mr. Olympia TV special that year against five other professional bodybuilders. After winning Mr. Olympia in 2018, Shawn worked on and appeared in his autobiographical movie, which was released in 2019.
