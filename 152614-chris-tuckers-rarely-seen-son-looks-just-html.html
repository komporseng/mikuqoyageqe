Chris Tucker's Rarely-Seen Son Looks Just like Him as They Attend Cannes Film Festival

Publish date: 2024-06-02

Chris Tucker brought his look-alike son to this year's Cannes Festival as they enjoyed the Quentin Tarantino premiere together.

The Tucker men attended the premiere of "Once Upon a Time in Hollywood," where the Quentin Tarantino movie made its debut. The "Rush Hour" star had his son with him the entire time, welcomed his son into the world back in 1998 with his then-wife Azja Pryor. Since then, their son Destin Christopher would rarely be seen out in the spotlight, and this occasion is one of the few times.

Father and Son Time

Aside from the Tarantino movie, the father-and-son duo also attended the Chopard Party together the week prior, and back in December during the Captain Planet Foundation's annual Benefit Gala in Atlanta. Destin was meant to be his dad's plus one during the occasion, as he moved to Atlanta from Southern California back in 2017 to study film at Morehouse College.

It seems there'll be another movie star in the Tucker family, but this time behind the scenes. With Dustin pursuing his passion for film, he will most definitely get a lot of guidance from his movie star dad himself.

Time for Family and Love

Chris Tucker has had a quiet couple of months as an actor, focusing his time on attending events and spending time with his family.

In 2015, Chris was engaged to Cynne Simpson, niece of "Video Soul" personality Donnie Simpson. They were preparing for their special day until the entire thing was called off. Now, he has allegedly found love again through Jarneen Chaney Brown.

Brown and Tucker have not been seen together since December 2018, with speculations circulating about whether or not the pair is still together.

Jarneen is an actress from Atlanta, who owns her own marketing firm named "Atlanta Injury Centers LLC," which specializes in marketing for attorneys and law firms. They have been together since 2017.
