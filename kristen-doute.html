Kristen Doute is an American model turned actress, entrepreneur, blogger, and clothing designer who is best known for being cast as Katie Richter in Behind Your Eyes (2011), Emma in Blood on Canvas (2013), Scarlet in Cats Dancing on Jupiter (2015), and as herself in Vanderpump Rules. Besides that, she is also noted for her appearance in various other shows such as The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills (2011-2013) and Watch What Happens: Live (2015-2018). On the other hand, Kristen is also noted for her apparel brand James Mae and her blog Vegiholic. With time, the beautiful Kristen has also amassed a huge fan base with more than 850k followers on Instagram, more than 50k followers on Facebook, and more than 300k followers on Twitter.

    Kristen Doute Facts

  • She was raised in Detroit and she later moved to Los Angeles to pursue a career in acting.
  • Growing up, Kristen wanted to be a marine biologist.
  • Kristen had turned into a vegetarian for good when she was 11 years old. She brought upon this change as she could not bear the fact that she was eating meat despite being an animal lover.
  • At Edsel Ford High School, Kristen took part in various stage productions organized there. She was also on her school’s cheerleading team during the year 1996. Back in 2003, Kristen became a cheerleading coach.
  • She started her clothing line, James Mae in 2014. 4 years later, she partnered with actress Megan Mattox in 2018. As of 2019, her apparel brand was flourishing due to the fact that the people found it to be a great way to contribute to the betterment of nature by making clothes from recycled plastic and fabric scraps.
  • In 2015, British restaurateur and entrepreneur Lisa Vanderpump fired Kristen from “SUR” on Vanderpump Rules. According to sources, Kristen had verbally asked her manager to leave her sight and later denied the accusations.

  • In 2017, Kristen Tweeted out to her fans that her sister was getting married in the following year.
  • She is obsessed with singer and songwriter Stevie Nicks and the rock band Fleetwood Mac. She even has a tattoo of Stevie’s handwriting on her wrist.
  • Kristen and TV personality Scheana Shay have the same kind of tattoos on their wrist.
  • She has a blog titled Vegiholic on which she uploads content such as recipes and benefits of maintaining a healthy vegetarian diet.
  • Kristen does consume alcohol. However, after a hangover, she prefers to hydrate herself with watermelon instead of french fries.
  • In June 2020, Kristen and Stassi Schroeder were fired by Bravo from Vanderpump Rules because of their past racist remarks against former co-star Faith Stowers.
