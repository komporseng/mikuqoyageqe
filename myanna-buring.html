Birth Name: Anna Margaretha My Rantapää

Place of Birth: Sundsvall, Sweden

Date of Birth: 22 September, 1979

Ethnicity: Finnish, possibly other

MyAnna Buring is an actress. She was born in Sundsvall, Sweden, and grew up in Oman and Kuwait. She is known for starring in the film The Descent (2005).

Her birth surname is Finnish.

She has one child.
