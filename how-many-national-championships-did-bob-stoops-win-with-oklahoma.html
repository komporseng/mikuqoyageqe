How many national championships did Bob Stoops win with Oklahoma?

Publish date: 2024-05-24

Bob Stoops, the esteemed head coach of the University of Oklahoma's football team, holds an illustrious record of achievement during his tenure. His prowess as a coach has been exemplified by the national championships won by the Oklahoma Sooners under his leadership.

The National Championships

2000 BCS National Championship

In the year 2000, Bob Stoops led the Oklahoma Sooners to a resounding victory in the BCS National Championship Game.

The Sooners, under his guidance, finished the season undefeated, outscoring their opponents by a combined score of 481–194.

This successful campaign culminated in a consensus national championship for the Oklahoma Sooners, marking a significant milestone in Bob Stoops' coaching career.

    2003, 2004, and 2008 BCS National Championship Game Appearances
