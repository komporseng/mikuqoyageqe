Lazar Angelov  Bio | Wiki

Lazar Angelov is a Bodybuilder, model, and basketball player. Before he started training, he played professional basketball for 10 years and he led his team in scoring for several seasons. Lazar joined the junior national team of Bulgaria at the age of 16. He then went to play with the National Basketball Association of Bulgaria.  Lazar runs a  YouTube channel where he posts training videos for his subscribers.

Lazar made a lasting career in the world of fitness and modeling after years of hard work and dedication. He rose to the top of his game and battled through many tough situations. Lazar achieved world recognition for he managed to get one of the best bodies in the industry. Through his online personal training, Lazar has created a successful business by helping others achieve their goals of obtaining their dream body.

Lazar Angelov Family

He was born and raised by his parents in Sofia, Bulgaria. Lazar’s inspiration to get fit and strong came from watching a lot of action movies. While growing up, Lazar emulated his big screen heroes. As a young not, he involved himself with basketball and weight training.  Lazar would complete two sessions of basketball training, one in the morning and the other one in the afternoon. After his service, Lazar got out of shape. As he was struggling with which direction he wanted to go in life, he got inspired to get serious about strength training and bodybuilding.

Since fitness was his second passion behind basketball, ILazar decided to put all his effort into it and that became his turning point. Though he has had some very tough periods in his life, he has gone through them all and is ready to overcome anything that life throws at him. To him, life is like a bench press, If one doesn’t push the weight, it will fall on him/her. This is why Lazar has to overcome each challenge life throws at him and move forward.
