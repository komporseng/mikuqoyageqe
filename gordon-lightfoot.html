Biography

Gordon Lightfoot was born in Orillia, Canada on November 17, 1938. Canadian singer-songwriter who recorded “If You Could Read My Mind.” He has won sixteen Juno Awards, among other honors. Some of his songs were recorded by Elvis Presley. He appeared on Orillia radio when he was a young soprano. He wrote the “Canadian Railroad Trilogy” for the CBC.
